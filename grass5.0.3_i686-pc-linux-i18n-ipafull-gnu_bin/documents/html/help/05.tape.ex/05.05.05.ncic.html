National Cartographic Information Center

(NCIC)

A good first step in determining the type and extent of digital data available for a specific region is to contact the National Cartographic Information Center (NCIC) and ask for a listing of data for your area. NCIC maintains an updated listing of all digital spatial data sources in the U.S., and they offer a search service for specified geographic regions. They can also supply indexes showing the status of coverage in your area.
