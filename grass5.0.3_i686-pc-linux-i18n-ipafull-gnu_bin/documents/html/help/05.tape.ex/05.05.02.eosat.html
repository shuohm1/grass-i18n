EARTH OBSERVATION SATELLITE CO. (EOSAT)

EOSAT: Multispectral - Satellite

Data Source: Earth Observation Satellite Co.(EOSAT) 4300 Forbes Blvd. Landham, MD. 20706 Phone: 1-800-367-2801

Data Format: MSS: BIP (thru 1/79)

BSQ or BIL corrected (1/79 - 5/81)
BSQ or BIL corrected or uncorrected (6/81 - present)
TM: BSQ or BIL corrected or uncorrected (4/84 - present) RBV: SSQ corrected (9/80 - present)

Scale/Resolution: MSS ~ 80m, TM ~ 30m, RBV ~ 80m

Data Coverage: Worldwide. Historical data may be obtained through EROS Data Center.

Data Description: Standard 4 band MSS, 7 band TM data, or RBV data. Since the LANDSAT system has been commercialized, users must sign a form stating that they will not copy or distribute the data without prior authorization from EOSAT.

Media: 9-track tape, 6250 or 1600 bpi.
